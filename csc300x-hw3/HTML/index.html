Introduction: Foodie-Camp

Welcome to Foodie-Camp, your gateway to campus culinary exploration! Craving a delicious meal without straying too far from campus? Look no further! Recommended Establishments

New York Pizza

Located at 337 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Hours of Operation:

  • Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday: 11:30am - 9:30pm
  • Saturday: 1pm - 9:30pm
  • Sunday: 1pm - 9pm
  • Wednesday: Closed

Specialities: Pay by the Slice! Get a cheese pizza slice for $3.85

All these pizzas are New York Styled

Personally, I've had the pizza slices and the cheese bites. They're pretty good.

Food Recommendations

8 pieces - $9.50

These are pretty much bites that have mac & cheese in them.

1 slice - $3.85

These slices are pretty big, they taste good too.

6 pieces - $9.99

These mozzarella sticks are delicious. They go well as a side when eating pizza.

Timmy's Hot Chicken

Located at 1040 W Gate City Blvd, Greensboro, NC 27403

Hours of Operation:

  • Tuesday - Sunday: 11am - 9pm
  • Monday: Closed

The meals here are Nashville styled, just like Dave's Hot Chicken. Although, this place is locally owned.

This location is the 2nd Timmy's Hot Chicken to open. Furthermore, it recently just opened in December.

Personally, I've been here once but the food is really good. It reminds of Dave's Hot Chicken.

Something cool that they have is the Timmy's Challenge! You basically have to eat 1 dish of hellfire wings under 10mins.

Food Recommendations

1 sandiwich - $12.00

You can pick between a chicken tender or thigh that'll be sandiwiched.

I had this sandiwich when I visited. It contains comeback sauce, bacon, and american cheese.

1 round - $4.00

These crinkle fries are pretty good. They're a good side to go with the sandiwich.

1 dish - $5.00

I haven't had this yet. It looks delicious, my friend said that it was good.

Jimmy John's

Located at 411 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Hours of Operation:

  • Sunday - Thurday: 10:30am - 1am
  • Friday - Saturday: 10:30am - 2am

These sandiwiches are cold sandiwiches. They have no hot sandiwiches.

They offer a loyalty points program. Usually, after ordering your first sandiwich online, you get the next one free as an offer.

Food Recommendations

1 sandiwich - $8.75

It contains turkey breast, smoked-ham, and provolone cheese.

I usually order this sandiwich with bacon and jalapanos.

1 sandiwich - $8.75

It contains double salami, double capocollo & provolone.

This sandiwich is pretty good. Although it lacks turkey meat.

1 sandiwich - $8.75

It contains bacon, ham & provolone, pickle, and mayo.

This sandiwich is pretty good. It sort of like a Cuban sandiwich, hence the name of the sandiwich.
